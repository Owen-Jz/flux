01 — About

What is Flux?

Flux is a modern task management platform built for teams who want to move fast without losing clarity.

The Problem We Solve

Teams struggle with scattered communications, missed deadlines, and lack of visibility into who's doing what. Traditional task tools are either too simple to handle real workflows or so complex that they become projects themselves.

Flux provides the right balance: visual boards that make status obvious at a glance, smart task decomposition that breaks complex work into actionable pieces, and seamless collaboration that keeps everyone aligned.

Our Core Promise

"Flux helps teams collaborate effortlessly and ship faster"

We believe task management should feel natural, not like administrative overhead. Every feature is designed to reduce friction and amplify focus.

How Flux Works

1. Workspaces

Create dedicated spaces for each team or project. Invite members, set permissions, and keep everything organized by context.

2. Boards

Visual Kanban boards let you see work status at a glance. Drag tasks between columns, customize workflows, and track progress instantly.

3. Tasks

Rich task cards with assignees, priorities, due dates, subtasks, and descriptions. Everything you need to execute and track work.

Key Capabilities

AI-Powered

Smart Task Decomposition

Input a complex deliverable and Flux's AI breaks it into actionable subtasks automatically. Save hours of planning and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Real-time

Live Collaboration

See changes as they happen. Comments, mentions, and notifications keep everyone in the loop without endless meetings or status update requests.

Flexible

Customizable Workflows

Your process, your columns. Flux adapts to how your team actually works, not the other way around. Start simple, evolve as you grow.

Scalable

Workspace Management

Admin controls, member roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer), and billing management built for teams of all sizes from startups to enterprises.
